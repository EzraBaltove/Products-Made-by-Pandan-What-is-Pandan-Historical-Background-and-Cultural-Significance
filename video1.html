PANDAN BASKET

Step by Step Process

1. Harvest the pandan leaves with a bolo knife or any sharp tool.

2. Dethorn the pandan leaves using a sharp knife.

3. Split the pandan leaves into different widths.

4. Drying and sorting the bundling.

5. Press the pandan leaves using a round wood mounted on a platform. Push the presser back and forth.

6. Manually weave the pandan leaves, weave them alternately.

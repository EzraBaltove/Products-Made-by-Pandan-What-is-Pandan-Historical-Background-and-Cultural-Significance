Step by Step Process

1. Harvest the Pandan leaves from your sources.

2. Boil the Pandan leaves for preparation for the drying procedure then, after it the drying procedure begins.

3. After drying the harvested pandan leaves, they will be pressed using the "Pang-ilo" to make them softer and easier to utilize while weaving..

4. Cut the pandan leaves to the desired width. The width depends on the product you will manufacture.

5.After getting the desired width of the pandan leaves, the "Mag lala" begins weaving. The most common pattern used is the "Up and Down Pattern."

6. After the weaving process, or the "Mag lala," the excess leaves can be cut.
